Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of medical professionals and AI researchers, AI Health Engine was born from a shared vision to democratize healthcare through technology.

We recognized the growing gap between healthcare demand and provider capacity, and set out to create intelligent solutions that could scale to meet global needs.

Today, our platform serves over 1 million users worldwide, with AI models trained on more than 10 million medical cases.
